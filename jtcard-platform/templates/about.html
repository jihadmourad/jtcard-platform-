About JTCard

We're revolutionizing professional networking by making digital business cards accessible, beautiful, and powerful for everyone.

Our Story

JTCard was born from a simple observation: traditional business cards are outdated, wasteful, and ineffective in our digital world. Founded in 2023, we set out to create a platform that would transform how professionals connect and share their information.

What started as a small team of designers and developers has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of professionals worldwide. We believe that networking should be seamless, sustainable, and smart.

Today, JTCard empowers professionals across industries to create stunning digital business cards that not only look great but also provide valuable insights into their networking effectiveness.

Our Mission & Vision

We're committed to transforming professional networking through innovative technology and sustainable practices.

Our Mission

To empower professionals worldwide with innovative digital business card solutions that enhance networking effectiveness while promoting environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for professional digital identity, making traditional paper business cards obsolete while creating meaningful connections.

Our Purpose

We believe that every professional deserves tools that reflect their expertise and values. We're here to make networking more effective, sustainable, and accessible.
