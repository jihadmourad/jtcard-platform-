Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about JTCard and our services.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24 hours. Live chat and phone support are available for immediate assistance during business hours.

What are your support hours?

Our live chat and phone support are available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. Email support is monitored 24/7 with responses within 24 hours.

Do you offer technical support for integrations?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support for all integrations and API implementations. Our technical team can assist with setup, troubleshooting, and optimization.

Can I schedule a demo or consultation?

Absolutely! We offer personalized demos and consultations to help you understand how JTCard can best serve your needs. Use the "Schedule a Call" option above to book a meeting.

Is there a knowledge base or documentation available?

Yes, our comprehensive Help Center includes detailed documentation, video tutorials, step-by-step guides, and troubleshooting resources to help you get the most out of JTCard.
