Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create my first digital business card?

Simply sign up for a free account, click "Create Card" and follow our easy step-by-step wizard. You can have your professional digital business card ready in under 5 minutes.

Is JTCard Platform really free?

Yes! Our basic plan is completely free and includes everything you need to create and share professional digital business cards. We also offer premium plans with advanced features.

Can I update my card information after sharing it?

Absolutely! One of the biggest advantages of digital business cards is that you can update your information anytime, and all shared cards will automatically reflect the changes.

How do people view my digital business card?

You can share your card via QR code, direct link, email, or social media. Recipients don't need to download any app - they can view your card directly in their web browser.

What analytics can I track?

You can see how many people viewed your card, when they visited, which links they clicked, and geographic data about your viewers. This helps you understand your networking impact.

Is my data secure with JTCard Platform?

Yes, we take security seriously. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure servers, and are fully GDPR compliant. Your data is safe and private with us.
