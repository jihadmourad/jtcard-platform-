About JTCard Platform

Revolutionizing professional networking with innovative digital business cards

Our Mission

At JTCard Platform, we believe that professional networking should be seamless, sustainable, and impactful. Our mission is to transform the way professionals connect by providing cutting-edge digital business card solutions that are both environmentally friendly and technologically advanced.

Founded in 2024, we recognized the need for a modern approach to business networking. Traditional paper business cards are often lost, forgotten, or thrown away. Digital business cards solve these problems while offering enhanced features like real-time updates, analytics, and multimedia content.
